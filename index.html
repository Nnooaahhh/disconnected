About Us

We're a bunch of rock enthusiasts from the vibrant music scene in New Jersey. Our love for rock 'n' roll brought us together, and now we're here to share our passion with you. Our journey as a band is one of shared experiences, late-night jam sessions, and the thrill of discovering new sounds. We've all been influenced by different rock legends, from the guitar riffs of Hendrix to the raw power of Nirvana. What sets us apart? We're not your typical band - we're a close-knit group of friends who live for those electrifying moments when we create music together. As a cover band, we take classic rock tracks and the latest hits and give them our unique twist, blending different styles to create something fresh and exciting. Join us on this rock 'n' roll journey. Whether you're a lifelong rock fan or new to the genre, we're here to take you on a musical ride you won't soon forget. Get ready to jump, dance, and sing along with us because the stage is set, and the lights are about to go up!
